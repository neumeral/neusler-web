Editorial Workflows

Managing a team of writers and editors can quickly get out of hand. Even for a small editorial team, managing your workflow can help streamline the process and track tasks and assignees.

A typical article lifecycle involves - research, multiple iterations of drafts, reviews, seo optimizations, publishing, social media distribution etc. Neusler can manage multiple teams and roles, that ensures that content moves through the right people leading to content publishing.

  • Email notifications, at the right time in the workflow.
  • Configurable roles and permissions. Content moderation, with comments and approvals.
  • Flexible and customizable workflows, can be as complex as multi chain approvals based on conditions, and attributes.
  • Content previews and revisions.
